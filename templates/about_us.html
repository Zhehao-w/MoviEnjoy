About Us

As a movie fan, do you often browse the latest movie list but find out there is nothing that really interests you? Do you want to share your own feelings with everyone other than just friends? Would you like to meet those who have similar tastes with you?


In the era of entertainment, there are more and more movies coming out every minute, while its harder and harder for us to make personalized choices. The media has made blockbusters everywhere for young and old, women and men, but it is not aware of what is suitable for a group of people just like you. No matter how fair and diligent the expert review writers are, their help cannot be effective for everyone. A recommendation from like-minded friends not only convey their true feelings, but also include judgments out of their tastes. Unfortunately, even you try to get suggestions from all of your friends and family, what they have seen are still limited, and the people with the most similar tastes are often strangers.


We, founders of MoviEnjoy, want to provide a platform for you to filter the other “YOU” from thousands of people and share your opinions with “YOU”. We are not aimed at a certain group of audience and strive to be inclusive. No matter who you are and what you like, we are glad to help you to get to know confidants and find more interesting movies through them.
